Rose & Cardamom Artisan Bread

A savory, uniquely aromatic loaf

üçû Method

  1. Mix: Combine water, honey, vanilla, rose water, and ground petals. Add yeast, wait 5 minutes. Add flour, salt, and oil. Mix until shaggy. Cover and rest 1 hour.
  2. Stretch & Fold: 4 sets, 30 minutes apart. Wet hands, stretch each side up and over the center.
  3. Bulk Ferment: Until doubled, 3-5 hours. Watch closely after hour 3 if using 7g yeast.
  4. Shape: Stretch dough into rectangle. Brush with water. Sprinkle spice mixture and petals. Fold in thirds, shape into batard. Place seam-up in floured banneton.
  5. Cold Proof: Cover and refrigerate 5-12 hours (no longer).
  6. Bake: Preheat Dutch oven at 450¬∞F for 30 minutes. Turn dough onto parchment, score, lower into pot. Bake 20 minutes covered, 15-20 minutes uncovered until deep golden.
  7. Glaze & Finish: Warm honey and water together. Brush onto hot loaf immediately. Press whole petals onto glazed surface.
  8. Cool: 1 hour minimum before slicing.
